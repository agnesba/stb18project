What is STB18

STB18 is on online platform designed for users by users.
The aim is to filter and collect relevant tweets in Korean with the correct English translations. The websites aims to redirect the experience on Twitter onto a specialised platform where the main information will reach clear and sound. By filtering the tweets and categorizing them the website will elimate some of the issues inherent to the other platforms such as mistranslations, fake and general oversaturation of information. 
Dedicated to BTS the platform will keep you uptodate with the latest tweets and make it easier to search and follow hashtags
